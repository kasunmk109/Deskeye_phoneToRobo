## 😢 When You're Not Visible

**Your DeskEye misses you!**

### **What Happens:**
1. **No face detected** for 15+ seconds
2. **Eyes close** (sleep mode)
3. **Mouth shows sad** expression
4. **Text displays:** "Where are you? 😢"
5. **Status shows:** "Missing you…"

### **How to Wake It:**
1. **Move back to camera**
2. **Eyes open instantly**
3. **Happy greeting appears**
4. **Normal mode resumes**
